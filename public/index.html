Now for a reality check

Creating new products is messy. Mistakes will be made. You will prioritize the wrong features and we will ship the occasional bug to production. But that’s okay. Perfection is not our goal.

Nor is our goal to build exactly what you have in mind. Why? Because successful products depend on healthy debate among business, design, and engineering.

Our goal is to help you create active users. Users excited to use your product. Users that will rave about it to their friends.
